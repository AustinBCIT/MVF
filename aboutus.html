Our Mission

We're providing an application that will allow consumers to search for recipes, with specific or left-over ingredients, and have a dashboard consumers can interact to manage their inventory. Adding to that is informing the user about ways to extend their ingredients with our useful tips & tricks.

Our Beliefs

Our beliefs that we can do more to reduce our food waste. We want to take that initial first step to work towards that goal of reducing food waste.
